updatED Intro - WSU COE

Mar. 10, 2023 - updatED is a WSU College of Education weekly newshow covering all important news across all four WSU campuses related to student and faculty work within the college. I was tasked with creating a "news-style" intro that could be used to lead into each weekly edition.

The Athletes Who Grabbed the Bag - SPJ Regional Winner

December 14, 2022 - A retrospective mini-documentary covering NCAA athletes and programs who were mired in scandal of taking illegal payments, and a reflection on the impact this has had on collegiate sports in the wake of NIL adoption. This documentary was recognized by the Society of Professional Journalists for Best Use of Multimedia against other applicants from Alaska, Oregon, Washington, Idaho, and Montana.
