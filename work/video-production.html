ROAR | Crimson & Gray

May 5, 2023 - A video package documenting the ROAR programs' raising of Ole Crimson at the WSU Spring Football game. The students also got to go onto the field in the second half as the school honored the work of the program over the stadium PA.

Nilesh Sheriff Ride-Along | WSU ROAR

March 29, 2023 - Nilesh is a ROAR student who loves to ride along with the police and fire departments. This video package covers his trip with the Latah Country Sheriff's Department, and all the cool technology they have at their disposal.

ROAR in the Workforce | STEPHANIE TRAVER

February 13, 2023 - Stephanie Traver is a ROAR student working with the on-campus Mexican food restaurant, Carlita's. She prepares peppers and other food items for cooking, cleans the kitchen, and maintains a crucial asset to the Carlita's team.

The Athletes Who Grabbed the Bag - SPJ Regional Winner

December 14, 2022 - A retrospective mini-documentary covering NCAA athletes and programs who were mired in scandal of taking illegal payments, and a reflection on the impact this has had on collegiate sports in the wake of NIL adoption. This documentary was recognized by the Society of Professional Journalists for Best Use of Multimedia against other applicants from Alaska, Oregon, Washington, Idaho, and Montana.

ROAR x WSU Baseball Lentil Cook Off 2022

September 27, 2022 - The ROAR program was invited to the second annual ROAR and WSU Baseball Team Lentil Cookoff, when ROAR students and baseball players teamed up to make the best dish using lentils in their ingredients.
